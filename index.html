Step-by-Step

Here's basically how it works:

  • Choose a Puzzle – Select a quick maze, logic riddle, or reaction challenge.

  • Solve It – These aren't marathon puzzles - we're talking quick 30-second brain teasers here.

  • Unlock Island Pieces – Nail the puzzle and boom - new island territory opens up.

  • Complete Regions – Each island region has unique themes and puzzle types.

  • Explore & Progress – Discover new zones, increase difficulty, and collect rewards.

Puzzle Types

  • Mazes – Navigate through small labyrinths.

  • Logic Puzzles – Pattern recognition, sequences, matching tasks.

  • Reaction Challenges – Tap-based speed tasks.

  • Memory Mini-Games – Remember paths, colors, or symbols.

  • Shape / Spatial Puzzles – Rearranging or connecting shapes.

  • Rotation / Swipe Puzzles – Quick movement-based actions.
